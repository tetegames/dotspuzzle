DOTS PUZZLE

Learn Best though Hard Fun

a challenging and minimal dot to dot measure puzzle game with handcrafted levels and aesthetic design




Reveal hand crafted picture and amazing story in each puzzle by connecting all pairs of dots that are separated by one or two target distances

Warning: some of the puzzle can be extreme hard.

Designed exclusively for the iOS devices and carefully crafted to let you explore imagination and learn amazing stories that make you suppried.

Features


  • 54 carefully handcrafted puzzles and amazing stories behind in 06 levels

  • Minimal and aesthetic visual design

  • Relaxing music and sound design

  • Clean and intuitive user interface

  • Exclusive for iOS

  • Game Center integration with Leaderboard and Achievements

  • No In-App Purchases and No ads
